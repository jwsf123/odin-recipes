Lotus Biscoff American Pancakes

Description

There's no better way to start your morning than with these delicious, fluffy American-style pancakes! They're really easy and quick to make and require just a few basic ingredients to whip up.

Flavoured with Lotus Biscoff spread and topped with crunchy biscuits, these pancakes may taste really naughtybut they're actually low on calories and slimming friendly!

Ingredients

  • 40g instant oat cereal
  • 100ml skimmed milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 2 tbsp Lotus Biscoff spread
  • 2 tsp low-calorie caramel syrup
  • low-calorie cooking spray
  • 1 Lotus Biscoff biscuit, crushed

Steps

  1. Add the instant oat cereal, milk and eggs to a bowl and whisk until combined.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of Lotus Biscoff spread and the caramel syrup and mix again. Leave to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Spray a frying pan with low-calorie cooking spray and place over a medium heat. When the pan is hot, spoon 2 tbsp of mixture into the pan and spread into little pancake shapes. Don't spread the mixture out too far as you want them to be nice and fluffy. It's best to cook two pancakes at once, if your pan is big enough.
  4. Cook the first side for 2 minutes until little bubbles appear on the surface, then flip over to cook the other side The pancakes should be a golden colour.
  5. Once the first batch is cooked, stack them on a plate and cover in foil to keep them warm.
  6. Move onto the next batch until all of the mixture is used up.
  7. Spread a little of the rest of the Lotus Biscoff spread onto each pancake.
  8. Stack them up and top with the crushed biscuit.
