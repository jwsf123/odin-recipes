Jammy Dodger French Toast

Description

Adorable heart-shpaed French toast with a fruity jam filling, dusted with sweet cinnamon. Perfect for that special someone - if you can bear to hand it over that is!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of wholemeal bread
  • 3 tbsp reduced-sugar strawberry jam
  • 2 eggs, beaten with a fork
  • 10ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 2 tbsp granulated sweetener
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • low-calorie cooking spray
  • 1 tsp icing sugar
To accompany
  • 4 tbsp fat-free Greek yoghurt sweetened with 1 tsp granulated sweetener
  • 4 sliced strawberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200C (fan 180C/gas mark 6).
  2. Using a 12cm-wide heart-shpaed cutter, cut all the pieces of bread into heart shapes.
  3. Using a 5cm circle cutter, cut out a circle on two of the slices of bread. Discard the excess bread.
  4. Spread half the jam on the two plain, heart-shaped slices of bread, then place the slices with the circles cut out on top.
  5. In a deep dish add the beaten egg and milk along with 1 tbsp of the granulated sweetener and 1 tsp of the cinnamon. Mix well.
  6. Carefully dip the bread into the egg mix, coating both sides.
  7. Spray a frying pan with low-calorie cooking spray and fry the bread for 5 minutes on both sides until golden brown.
  8. Place the bread on a baking tray with the circle side face up, add the remaining jam to the circle and put in the oven for 10 minutes.
  9. In a small bowl mix the remaining cinnamon and granulated sweetener together and sprinkle over the cooked toast.
  10. Serve with a light dusting of icing sugar.
